What people think about us

Megan Fox

Stayed 18 Nov, 2019

It was very nice hotel with cleanliness. Staff behavior was good and polite. They welcome us very well. Issue was only that Lift was not in working and we were allotted to 3rd floor and amenities articles were in corner of gallery which were giving bad feeling. Breakfast was good and support of the staff was also very nice. Location is not good as per atmosphere, it is very nearby most of the popular places but self location in a narrow street is not good. Overall it was a good experience and could recommend.

Alfie Doner

Stayed 15 Dec, 2018

We have been to this hotel several times, each time returning to it with joy. The hotel staff is friendly, there is a Russian-speaking employee, the rooms are clean, although the rooms are not very large. I advise you to take a room with a fireplace (it is larger and creates a romantic atmosphere). I liked the kitchen both in the hotel restaurant and when ordering to the room. The hotel can only be entered by hotel guests with a card, which is an undoubted advantage. The hotel has a very convenient location: a 2-3 minute walk to Buckingham Palace, within walking distance to my beloved St. James's Park, to the metro station. The last time we were in this hotel, our stay was overshadowed by terrible noise (including at night) from the street, as some renovation work is underway under the windows of the hotel, in connection with which we can sleep (or just relax and unwind) ) was simply impossible.

Jack Alrik

Stayed 21 Oct, 2019

Located in central London, just 5 minutes from the Green Park Underground, the whole of central London is at your disposal. You can walk to London Eye, Big Ben, Buckingham Palace. This is a huge plus. The hotel itself is very cozy inside, in the English style. Rooms are small, bed (soft and comfortable), wardrobe and little space around. The bathtub also does not indulge in size. Probably just to sleep in these rooms exclusively. But in the morning, you get a wonderful breakfast, you order oatmeal, any eggs - everything is at the highest level. And the culmination is a huge plate of strawberries, raspberries, blackberries and blueberries. Believe me, in November, I - a man from a northern country, was greatly impressed. There is a Russian speaking girl at the reception.
